> 
> SmartBear was acquired by Francisco Partners in 2017\. Headquartered in the greater Boston area, it provides software development solutions to more than 15 million developers, testers and operational engineers in more than 24,000 organizations. SmartBear solutions support the fast-growing DevOps market, including innovative technologies around API lifecycle management, test automation, test management, performance optimization and collaboration. These solutions are used by leading organizations to accelerate the large-scale design, development and release of high-quality software.
> 
> Frank Roe, CEO of SmartBear, said: "On behalf of all the people in Smart Bear, I am very happy to welcome Vista as an investor. "The strong support of two leading private equity companies shows the great achievements of our products and employees so far, as well as the growth and demand of our market. As every company is rapidly transforming to digital priority, we see a sharp increase in the demand for SmartBear solutions from developers, testers and the whole organization. They focus on continuously delivering high-quality software at the speed required by the business. "
> 
> Smart Bear solutions span the entire software delivery life cycle, enabling organizations to reduce costs and speed up time to market. The capital investment in Vista SmartBear and the company's rich experience in working with world-class enterprise software companies and their leadership teams will enable SmartBear to expand and strengthen its solutions and identify opportunities for sustained organic and inorganic growth.
> 
> Michael Fosnaugh, co-director of Vista flagship fund and senior managing director of Vista, said: "SmartBear suite is the best-in-class DevOps solution. Its commitment to the open source community is the recognition of its product strength and enables the company to grow continuously in this fast-growing market. "We look forward to cooperating with Frank and SmartBear teams and Francisco Partners to continue to provide innovative and valuable solutions to software teams around the world."
> 
> Brian Decker, partner and Evan Daar, head of Francisco Partners commented, "Since we invested in SmartBear, the company has established an impressive record as a market-leading platform in the huge and growing DevOps market."
> 
> David Golob, chief investment officer of Francisco Partners, added: "In the past few years, Frank and SmartBear team have shown great ability in accelerating organic and inorganic growth, and we are glad to continue our journey with Vista. Evercore serves as the chief financial advisor of Francisco Partners and SmartBear, and Shea & Company also serves as an advisor. Paul Hastings Ellis is the legal advisor of SmartBear, and kirkland & Ellis is the legal advisor of Vista.
